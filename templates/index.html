About

Derpbladet is a python micro-micro-framework for taking a local copy of a website and translate it to derps. The aggressive derpifying of the html document will often lead to target sites losing structural layout.

Download is available at https://github.com/jonatanfan/derpbladet. Demo application is running on Flask
